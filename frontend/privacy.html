Privacy Policy

FixItSelf does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All conversation data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never sent to our servers beyond the device and search parameters you provide to fetch repair results.

We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics services. Search queries are sent to public search engines (DuckDuckGo, Bing, Google, YouTube) to find publicly available repair content. No personally identifiable information is logged server-side.

If you have questions, contact the repository maintainer.
